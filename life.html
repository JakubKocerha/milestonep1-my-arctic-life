Life and people aren't easy up here. However, a vast area with lakes, mountains, and nobody around gives freedom of choice. It gives a range of possibilities and contrasts very rare and different to what and where most of us live in.

Winter time

Ice fishing

It might be tough to get fresh protein those times. However, skiing a snowshoeing through deep snow to remote frozen fishing lakes with water you may drink and fish in is worthy of doing and rewarding. Those are the lakes with the best fish in the world.

Find out more!
Snow camping

The period of no sun or low day light and extreme cold could be challenging. It's good to get out of the cave and spend some time in any nearby off-grid cabins or simply in a tent. One gets an instant chance to appreciate the beauty of simplicity.

Find out more!
Keeping mind healthy

While waiting for the spring sun to come back, there is a lot of time for indoor activities. I personally spend a lot of time reading books, making berry wines out of local fantastic and plentiful fruits, or sculpting wooden figures.

Find out more!
Meeting with mates

We all have to do something for a living. Most of us work in tourism. When we are free, we often meet and make a fire, bathe in an ice hole and go to the sauna, and do many other things at any temperature. You get used to it.

Find out more!

Summer time

Summer fishing

Fishing is my great passion and excellent food source. As soon as the snow melts, I love going fly fishing or fishing in the nearby ocean. As a licensed net fisherman, I set my nets in the lake of Torneträsk. That makes things more efficient. Then I smoke fish or freeze them for the winter.

Find out more!
Staying out of trails

If I really want untouched nature, I have to get far out of the common tourist trails. That kind of hiking rewards me with the only presence and sound of nature without any human interference and footprint.

Find out more!
Never forget

There are two things I cannot be without when out in the mountains in the summer, and it is coffee and mosquitoes. These little creatures are a really integral part of the summer life up here, no matter how annoying they could be. So better than fighting them, get used to it.

Find out more!
Berry picking

The late summer is the time of mists and colorful landscapes. I get nostalgic because the summer is saying goodbye. However, it is the time of berries and mushrooms too. It is time to pick them and preserve them. The winter is long, dark, and cold. Get ready, then!
