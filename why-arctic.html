Why living above the polar circle?

Childhood Dream

As a small boy, I was reading magazines for fishers and dreaming about Lofoten, a beautiful archipelago in the north of Norway. By coincidence, it happened that I moved in not that far from there in my thirties.

Out Of City

I used to live in many big European cities. A long time before I visited the Arctic for the first time, I had known that the sound of any bigger city resonating into the surroundings day and night isn't where I want to live my entire life.

Pristine Nature

The nature in the arctic isn't compromising. You have to respect it. It is full of varieties of animal and plant species. Water is clean and, in most cases, drinkable without any processing. The air is fresh and pollution difficult to be found.

Adventure

It might not be easy to find a concert hall or national galleries. On another side, you can stroll on accessible trails, go long-distance, climb, trail run, ski, paddle with a kayak, watch dancing northern lights or listen to the waterfalls and birds singing.
