Join me for an adventure!

2 Weeks off-grid in the wilderness!

What do you get?

I admit that nearly a decade in the Scandinavian arctic wilderness taught me a lot. My arctic journey will end soon. But before that, I want to spend a year in a remote area 50 km from a nearby village of Abisko and share with you a short time and what I have learned. You'll get a chance to spend 2 weeks in a remote wilderness in the Arctic where I'll guide you how to fish, distinguish edible plants, make fire without matches, perhaps meet a bear, make a sauna with a tarp, and a lot of other cool stuff, including how to survive in situations you don't normally expect and how to act that the impact of our presence in the nature doesn't cause damage. Note that this project is solely voluntary, and you pay all expenses necessary for your travel, and you do it at your own risk.

How to apply?

Write a short letter about you, what you want to achieve and what you wish to attain by joining this unusual journey.
